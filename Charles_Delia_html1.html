My Mom

My mother is the most beautiful woman you will ever meet. She's full of so much love and care; the type of person that could never hurt a fly. I admire her more than anything and she means the whole world to me. I want nothing more in my life than to be able to support her the way she has supported me

Even after going through so much hardship and pain, she still stands strong. Words cannot describe the amount of strength she holds despite so many things trying to bring her down.
